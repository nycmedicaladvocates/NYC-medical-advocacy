Last updated: Feb 2026

Step 1

Confirm what the document is
  • An EOB is not always a bill.
  • Look for date of service, facility, and account number.
  • Save photos of the full page.

Step 2

Ask about financial assistance
  • Call billing and ask for financial assistance applications.
  • Ask how to submit documents and how long decisions take.
NYC Health + Hospitals financial assistance

Step 3

Surprise bills and out of network issues
  • NY State has a dispute process (IDR) for certain cases.
NY DFS IDR

Step 4

Denials, appeals, and advocacy
  • Free advocates can help with denials and complex billing issues.

Safety and disclaimer
