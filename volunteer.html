Volunteer with NYC Medical Advocates

Help people in NYC navigate healthcare, appointments, paperwork, and public resources. No prior experience required — we provide simple guidance and support.

  • Help people find low-cost clinics and services
  • Assist with scheduling appointments
  • Guide patients through forms and paperwork
  • Connect people to trusted public resources
  • Support seniors, families, and community members

How it works

  • Flexible — volunteer when you are available
  • Remote or in-person options (depending on role)
  • Simple scripts and guidance provided
  • Focus on navigation support — not medical advice
