Bills and insurance

Help understanding bills, letters, and next steps

This page is a starting point for people dealing with medical bills, insurance letters, denials, or confusing paperwork. We provide navigation support only. We do not provide legal advice or medical advice.

What may help

Useful information to gather

  • The bill or letter itself
  • Account number
  • Date of service
  • Insurance card, if you have one
  • Any denial or explanation of benefits

What we can do

Navigation support only

  • Help identify who to call first
  • Help prepare a short list of questions
  • Help organize documents before the call
  • Help point you to official assistance or dispute resources

Hospital billing office

Often the first place to call for a hospital bill.

Financial assistance

May be available through hospital systems like NYC Health + Hospitals.

Consumer dispute help

State and public-interest resources may help with certain billing issues.
