Free healthcare navigation help in NYC

Clear next steps when healthcare feels overwhelming.

We help New Yorkers figure out where to start, who to call, what to ask, and what documents to prepare. We support navigation for clinics, appointments, bills, low-cost options, recovery resources, transportation, portals, and trusted public programs.

100% free healthcare navigation support • Not medical advice • Not for emergencies

Non-urgent help

Call or text 201-822-5428

Use Start Here, call, text, or the online form for non-urgent help.

Who this is for

People who need a clearer next step

  • New Yorkers who are uninsured or not sure where to begin
  • Patients and families dealing with bills, denials, or confusing paperwork
  • Seniors, caregivers, and people who need help with appointments or patient portals
  • People looking for trusted public resources instead of guessing online
  • People who need help finding low-cost care, recovery support, or language access

What we do

We provide practical navigation support. We do not diagnose, recommend treatment, prescribe medications, or replace emergency care.

How it works

One practical step at a time

  1. Start with the page that matches your problem.
  2. Use the form, call, or text for non-urgent help.
  3. We help identify the next step and share trusted resources.

Why this program exists

Healthcare navigation is hard

People often get stuck between clinics, insurance questions, transportation, bills, forms, portals, and follow-up instructions. This program exists to reduce confusion and help people move forward using trusted public and nonprofit resources.
