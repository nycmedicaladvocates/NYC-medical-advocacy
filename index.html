Free healthcare navigation help in NYC

Clear next steps when healthcare feels overwhelming.

We help people figure out where to start, who to call, what to ask, and what documents to prepare. We support navigation for care, appointments, bills, paperwork, low-cost options, transportation, medication costs, and trusted public resources.

100% free healthcare navigation support • Not medical advice • Not for emergencies

Who this is for

People who need a clearer next step
  • People who are uninsured or not sure where to begin
  • Patients and families dealing with bills or confusing paperwork
  • Seniors, caregivers, and community members navigating appointments or forms
  • People looking for trusted public resources instead of guessing

What we do

We provide practical navigation support. We do not diagnose, recommend treatments, prescribe medications, or replace emergency care.

How it works

One practical step at a time
  1. Start with the landing page or browse the directory.
  2. Call, text, or submit a non-urgent request form.
  3. We help identify the next step and share trusted resources.

Why this program exists

Healthcare navigation is hard

People often get stuck between clinics, insurance questions, transportation, bills, forms, and follow-up instructions. This program exists to reduce confusion and help people move forward using trusted public and nonprofit resources.
