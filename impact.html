Impact

What impact means for this program

The goal is not to replace care. The goal is to help people move forward when the system feels confusing: clearer next steps, better preparation, faster connection to the right resource, and safer redirection when something is urgent.

What we track

Program activity

  • Requests received by form, call, or text
  • Follow-ups completed
  • Common resource categories used
  • Volunteer participation and support capacity

Why it matters

Better first steps

  • People reach the right resource faster
  • People are better prepared for calls, visits, and paperwork
  • Families have a clearer plan for what to do next
  • Urgent situations are redirected to 911 or 988 more clearly

Examples of support

De-identified examples

  • Helping someone identify the best official starting point for uninsured or lower-cost care
  • Helping a patient organize a bill, account number, and date of service before calling about a billing issue
  • Helping a family prepare a short list of questions before a clinic follow-up
  • Helping someone identify where to ask for language access or interpreter support
