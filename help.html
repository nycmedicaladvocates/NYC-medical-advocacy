What we help with

Navigation support only

  • Finding the right starting point for care
  • Preparing for calls, visits, and paperwork
  • Understanding official programs and public resources
  • Finding help for bills, transportation, medication costs, and language access

What we do not do

Important limits

  • We do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations
  • We do not replace a doctor, clinic, or hospital
  • We do not handle emergencies
  • We do not promise eligibility, coverage, or appointment availability
