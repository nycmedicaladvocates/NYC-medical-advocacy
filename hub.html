Who we are

A free community-based program

We are not a clinic, hospital, insurance company, or emergency service. We are a volunteer navigation program that helps people understand options and identify the next practical step.

What we do

Practical help when the system feels confusing

  • Identify where to start
  • Find the right office, clinic, or resource
  • Prepare questions for a call or appointment
  • Organize bills, letters, and forms
  • Connect people to trusted public resources
