About

A free volunteer healthcare navigation program in NYC

NYC Medical Advocates helps people figure out where to start, who to call, what to ask, and what documents to prepare. We focus on practical next steps using official and reputable public resources whenever possible.

100% free healthcare navigation support • Not medical advice • Not for emergencies

What we do

Practical navigation support

  • Help identify where to start
  • Help find the right office, clinic, or public program
  • Help prepare questions before a call or appointment
  • Help organize bills, letters, and forms
  • Help connect people to trusted resources

What we do not do

Important boundaries

  • We do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment
  • We do not replace doctors, nurses, or emergency services
  • We do not guarantee eligibility, appointments, or outcomes
  • We do not provide emergency response

Why this exists

Healthcare navigation is a real barrier

Many people get stuck between clinics, insurance questions, transportation, medication costs, bills, paperwork, and follow-up instructions. This program exists to help reduce confusion and give people a clearer next step.

Resource standards

Official and reputable sources first

We prioritize official and highly reputable sources, including NYC agencies, NY State resources, NYC Health + Hospitals, and recognized public-interest organizations. Program details can change, so people should always confirm current information directly with the provider.
