About

A free volunteer program for healthcare navigation in NYC

NYC Medical Advocates helps people navigate the healthcare system: where to start, who to call, what to ask, and what documents to prepare. We focus on practical next steps using official and reputable public resources whenever possible.

100% free healthcare navigation support • Not medical advice • Not for emergencies

What we do

Practical navigation support
  • Help identify the best place to start
  • Help find the right office, clinic, or public program
  • Help prepare questions before a call or appointment
  • Help organize bills, letters, and forms
  • Help connect people to trusted resources

What we do not do

Important boundaries
  • We do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment
  • We do not replace doctors, nurses, or emergency services
  • We do not guarantee eligibility, appointments, or outcomes
  • We do not provide emergency response

Why this exists

Healthcare navigation is a real barrier

Many people struggle to navigate clinics, bills, insurance questions, transportation, medication costs, and confusing paperwork. This program exists to help reduce that confusion and give people a clearer next step.

Resource standards

Official and reputable sources first

We prioritize official and highly reputable sources, including NYC agencies, NY State resources, NYC Health + Hospitals, and recognized public-interest organizations. Programs and eligibility can change, so people should confirm details directly with the provider.
