Privacy and boundaries

Important program limits and privacy expectations

NYC Medical Advocates provides free healthcare navigation support only. We help people understand options, prepare questions, and identify next steps. We do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency response.

Navigation support only • Not medical advice • Not emergency services

Program scope

What we can do
  • Share official and reputable resources
  • Help people prepare questions and documents
  • Help identify the next practical step
  • Support non-urgent navigation needs

Out of scope

What we cannot do
  • Medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations
  • Emergency services or crisis response beyond directing to 911 or 988
  • Promises about eligibility, appointments, coverage, or outcomes
  • Legal advice

Privacy approach

Share only what is necessary

If you contact us, only share the information needed to understand your question and help identify the next step. Do not send highly sensitive information unless it is truly necessary for your request.

Safety first

Urgent situations

If there is immediate danger, severe symptoms, or an urgent emergency, call 911. If there is a mental health crisis, call or text 988. The online request form is not monitored in real time.
