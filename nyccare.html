NYC Care

A starting point for New Yorkers without affordable coverage

NYC Care is a program from NYC Health + Hospitals for New Yorkers who do not qualify for or cannot afford health insurance. This page is here to help you understand where to start and what kind of questions to prepare. We do not decide eligibility. We do not provide medical advice.

What NYC Care is

A healthcare access program, not insurance

NYC Care can help connect eligible New Yorkers to care through NYC Health + Hospitals. It is often useful for people who are uninsured and want a clearer path into the healthcare system.

What we can do

Navigation support only
  • Help identify the official starting point
  • Help prepare questions before you call or apply
  • Help organize documents and next steps
  • Help compare NYC Care with other official coverage options

Helpful questions to ask

Before you contact the program
  • What documents should I bring?
  • Where do I go to start enrollment?
  • What care locations are available through NYC Health + Hospitals?
  • If I might qualify for insurance, should I also look at NY State of Health?

Important note

Use the official program for current details

Program details, eligibility rules, and locations can change. Always confirm current information with the official NYC Care page or NYC Health + Hospitals directly.
